How it works

From sender to your
door in 3 steps

No banks. No wire transfers. A background-checked courier picks up your cash and delivers it safely, with every movement tracked live on your phone.

01
Package Sealed & Logged
The sender seals cash or an item in a tamper-evident envelope. Our system generates a unique tracking ID and assigns a verified local courier near you.
02
Live GPS Tracking
From pickup to drop-off, you and the recipient track the courier's location in real time. Text and push notifications at every milestone — no guessing required.
03
Confirmed Delivery
The recipient confirms via a one-time PIN on the courier's app. Both sides receive instant confirmation and a digital receipt. Escrow funds are released immediately.
